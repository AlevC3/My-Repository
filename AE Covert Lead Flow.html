1. AE Reviews the SQL Lead

Trigger: Lead assigned to AE | Owner: Account Executive
  • Navigate to the lead in the Old Salesforce instance
  • Assess lead validity and readiness for conversion
  • If more qualification is needed, return to ODR
Expected Result: Lead details available with AE
⬇

2. Determine if Lead Belongs in New Stack

Trigger: Lead review assessment | Owner: Account Executive
  • Evaluate market / region (e.g. Canada Commercial)
  • Confirm product or segment alignment
  • Check duplication or data quality issues
  • If valid → proceed
⬇

3. Flag Lead as New Stack Record

Trigger: Preconditions cleared | Owner: Account Executive
  • Locate “New Stack Record” checkbox on lead
  • Select checkbox
Expected Result: “Sync to New Stack” button appears
⬇

4. Execute Sync to New Stack

Owner: Account Executive
  • Click “Sync to New Stack”
  • System creates lead in new instance
  • Lead enters Sales Working (SWL) stage
  • Fields mapped (~800 → ~100)
  • Einstein Activity Capture enabled
Expected Result: Lead synced to New Stack
⬇

5. Lead Conversion Initiation

Trigger: Lead synced | Owner: Account Executive
  • Verify synced lead details
  • Assign lead to an owner (not a queue)
  • Click “Convert Lead”
Expected Result: Lead conversion initiated. AE continues work fully in New Stack.
